Common Questions Before You Reach Out

How does the free consultation work?

We come to your home, walk the outdoor space with you, discuss your vision and material preferences, take measurements, and provide a written estimate — all at no charge. There is no obligation and no pressure to move forward.

What areas do you serve in Greater Houston?

We serve Houston, Missouri City, Sugar Land, Katy, Pearland, Sienna, Manvel, Richmond, Rosenberg, Stafford, and surrounding Fort Bend County communities. If you are unsure whether we cover your neighborhood, just call (346) 551-8340 — we likely serve your area.

What services does Longhorn Hardscape offer?

We build patio covers, pergolas, outdoor kitchens, artificial turf and putting greens, fire features, custom stonework and masonry, driveways and concrete, complete outdoor living spaces, and exterior painting. We handle the full project from demolition through final cleanup.

How do I prepare for my consultation?

Think about how you want to use the space — entertaining, relaxing, kids and pets, cooking, etc. It also helps to have a rough idea of your budget range and any photos of styles you like. We will handle the rest: measurements, material recommendations, and a written estimate. No preparation is required — just be available to walk the space with us.

Can I see examples of your past work before committing?

Absolutely. Browse our project gallery to see completed hardscape and outdoor living projects across Greater Houston. Every photo is a real project we built — no stock images.
