Our Artificial Turf Installation Process

1

Free On-Site Consultation

We visit your property, measure the area, assess drainage, and discuss turf options — bringing samples so you can see and feel the difference.

2

Custom Design & Estimate

We design your layout and provide a detailed written estimate before we leave — covering all materials, base prep, and labor.

3

Site Preparation

We remove existing grass, grade the area for proper drainage, and install a compacted crushed stone base — the foundation that makes turf last.

4

Turf Installation

We lay the turf, secure all edges, seam panels invisibly, add infill, and power-brush the blades upright for a natural, lush appearance.

5

Final Walkthrough & Care Guide

We inspect every seam and edge, clean up completely, and walk you through the simple maintenance routine to keep your turf looking great for years.
