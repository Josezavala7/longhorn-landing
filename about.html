Built on Hard Work and Honest Craftsmanship

Longhorn Hardscape & Construction was founded with a simple belief: homeowners in the Houston area deserve an outdoor contractor they can actually trust. Someone who shows up on time, does what they say, builds it right the first time, and stands behind the work after the job is done.

We started in the Missouri City area and grew entirely through referrals — one satisfied client at a time. Every project we take on gets the same level of personal attention, whether it's a straightforward paver patio or a complete outdoor transformation with a kitchen, pergola, and turf lawn.

Today we serve all of Greater Houston and Fort Bend County, with a growing team of skilled craftsmen who share the same commitment to quality and client satisfaction that built this company from the ground up.
