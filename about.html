Our Story

We started with a simple belief: homeowners deserve a contractor they can truly trust.

Longhorn Hardscape & Construction was founded in the Missouri City area and has grown almost entirely through referrals—one satisfied client at a time. From the very beginning, our mission has been simple: show up when we say we will, communicate honestly, deliver exceptional craftsmanship, and stand behind every project long after the work is complete.

Whether we're installing a custom paver patio or creating a complete outdoor living space with a patio cover, pergola, outdoor kitchen, artificial turf, and fire features, every project receives the same level of care, attention to detail, and commitment to quality.

Today, we're proud to serve homeowners throughout Greater Houston and Fort Bend County with a growing team of skilled craftsmen who share the same values that built our company from the ground up—integrity, quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction. Our commitment to excellence isn't just a promise; it's the foundation of every project we build.
