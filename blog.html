About Longhorn Hardscape — Greater Houston's Outdoor Living Contractor

We are a hardscape and outdoor living contractor based in Missouri City, TX serving homeowners throughout Greater Houston and Fort Bend County. Our work covers patio installation, patio covers, pergolas, artificial turf, outdoor kitchens, fire features, driveways and concrete, custom stonework, and complete outdoor living spaces.

Houston's climate — hot summers, heavy clay soil, flash flooding, and the occasional freeze — creates unique challenges for outdoor construction. Patios need proper drainage and the right base depth to survive Fort Bend County clay. Patio covers need to handle both the sun load and the occasional tropical storm. Materials need to stand up to UV exposure without fading or cracking after a season or two. The articles on this blog are written from direct experience building in these conditions across Missouri City, Sugar Land, Pearland, Katy, Sienna, Manvel, Richmond, and Houston proper.
