How does Fort Bend County's clay soil affect hardscape projects?

Fort Bend County has notoriously heavy clay soil that expands and contracts with moisture changes. This means proper base preparation is critical for patios and driveways. We excavate deeper than the minimum, use a compacted crushed-stone base, and install the correct drainage slope so water moves away from the structure. This prevents shifting, cracking, and settling over time.

Do you offer artificial turf installation across Fort Bend County?

Yes. Artificial turf is one of our most popular services in Fort Bend County — both because of the water restrictions in many communities and because the clay soil makes it difficult to maintain natural grass evenly. We install turf for backyard lawns, putting greens, dog runs, and pool surrounds throughout the county.
